# Global Finance — Categories & Priority

## Categories
- MARKETS — Stock, bond, equity market moves
- MACRO — GDP, inflation, recession, economic outlook
- CENTRAL-BANK — Fed, ECB, BOJ, BOE rate decisions & statements
- EARNINGS — Corporate earnings, revenue, guidance
- COMMODITIES — Oil, gold, metals, agricultural
- FOREX — Currency moves, USD pairs, EM currencies
- IPO — New listings, SPACs, delistings
- POLICY — Fiscal policy, tax, trade, sanctions

## Priority Levels
- P1 MAJOR 🔴 — Rate decision, market crash/surge >3%, systemic risk event
- P2 SIGNIFICANT 🟠 — Major earnings beat/miss, central bank speech, recession signal
- P3 NOTABLE 🟡 — Sector move, commodity price shift, analyst upgrade/downgrade
- P4 FYI ⚪ — Minor data release, routine filing
