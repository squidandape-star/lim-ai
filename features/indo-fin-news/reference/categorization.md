# Indonesia Finance — Categories & Priority

## Categories
- IDX — Stock exchange, IHSG, listed companies
- BANKING — Perbankan, OJK ruling, NPL, credit
- RUPIAH — IDR exchange rate, BI rate, monetary policy
- COMMODITIES — CPO, coal, nickel, tin (Indonesia's key exports)
- STARTUP — Tech startups, unicorns, VC funding
- REGULATION — OJK, BI, Kemenkeu regulation, tax
- MACRO — GDP, inflation, trade balance, budget
- SOE — BUMN (state-owned enterprises) news

## Priority Levels
- P1 MAJOR 🔴 — BI rate decision, IHSG crash/surge >3%, systemic banking event
- P2 SIGNIFICANT 🟠 — Major IPO, OJK policy, significant rupiah move, unicorn funding
- P3 NOTABLE 🟡 — Sector update, commodity price impact on Indonesia, earnings
- P4 FYI ⚪ — Minor regulatory update, routine economic data
