# Cryptocurrency — Categories & Priority

## Categories
- BTC — Bitcoin price, network, halving, ETF, miners
- ETH — Ethereum upgrades, staking, L2s, EIP
- ALTCOIN — Solana, XRP, ADA, AVAX, other coins
- DEFI — Protocols, TVL, yield, stablecoins, DEX
- REGULATION — SEC, CFTC, MiCA, global crypto law
- EXCHANGE — CEX news (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, OKX)
- MACRO — Crypto correlation with macro, institutional flows
- HACK — Exploits, rug pulls, bridge hacks, losses

## Priority Levels
- P1 MAJOR 🔴 — BTC/ETH >10% move, major hack >$50M, landmark regulation
- P2 SIGNIFICANT 🟠 — Major exchange event, ETF approval/rejection, altcoin >20%
- P3 NOTABLE 🟡 — Protocol upgrade, medium hack, new exchange listing
- P4 FYI ⚪ — Minor price move, routine airdrop, small protocol update
